Discover Beaver Dam

A small city with a whole lot to see.

From sunset over the lake to fireworks on the Fourth, our town has its own rhythm — slower, friendlier, and worth the visit.

Parks & Lakeside

Waterfront trails, picnic pavilions, and one of the largest inland lakes in southeastern Wisconsin.

Sunsets & Seasons

Autumn color on the water, summer on the patio, winter lights downtown — we lean into every one of them.

Celebrations

Fireworks, parades, festivals and fairs — Beaver Dam shows up for its holidays and its neighbors.
