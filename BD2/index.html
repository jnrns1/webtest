in this issue —

Three good reasons to stop by.

There are more, of course. These just happen to be the ones most folks mention first.

01

Eat local, stay a while.

Corn on the cob in August. Cheese and brats all year. A supper-club Old Fashioned that tastes like it should. Our downtown and our chamber members take the kitchen seriously.

02

See the lake do its thing.

Beaver Dam Lake is one of the largest shallow lakes in the region — a whole sky of color at sundown, ice fishermen in winter, and something green and alive the rest of the year.

03

Show up for something.

A parade. A fireworks show. A Saturday market. A festival named after a pepper, because of course. Something's happening — and nobody minds if you're new.
