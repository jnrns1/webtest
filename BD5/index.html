PROGRAMS

Three programs that move the local economy.

Beyond networking, the Chamber operates flagship programs that develop leaders, align manufacturers, and build the next generation of Beaver Dam business owners.

PROGRAM I — LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT

Leadership Beaver Dam — a 9-month cohort that builds civic leaders.

Each year, ~25 emerging leaders from across the community spend a day a month immersed in city systems — government, healthcare, manufacturing, education, agriculture, public safety. By the end, you understand how the city actually works, and you've built relationships that last a career.

  • Monthly full-day immersion sessions
  • Capstone class project with civic impact
  • Lifetime alumni network of 400 + Beaver Dam leaders

PROGRAM II — INDUSTRY ALIGNMENT

Manufacturing Business Alliance — peer council for Dodge County manufacturers.

Beaver Dam is a manufacturing town. The MBA gives plant managers, owners, and HR leaders a place to compare notes on workforce, automation, supply chain, and apprenticeships — without the noise.

  • Quarterly peer roundtables
  • Workforce & apprenticeship pipeline coordination
  • Direct line to county economic development

PROGRAM III — NEXT GENERATION

Young Professional Network — for Beaver Dam's under-40 leaders.

Casual mixers, professional development, and community service for the people who'll run this town in ten years. YPN is also where the Chamber's annual 5 Under 40 awards come from.

  • Monthly social + learning meetups
  • Mentor matching with senior chamber members
  • Pathway to Leadership Beaver Dam & the board
