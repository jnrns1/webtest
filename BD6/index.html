A LETTER FROM THE CHAMBER

We exist to make this a place where business is worth doing.

The Beaver Dam Area Chamber of Commerce is a local business organization that works to create an engaging community with prosperous businesses. We are funded by the generosity of our members — the bankers, manufacturers, restaurateurs, retailers, and trades that make this town function.

Whether you are visiting, exploring a move to the area, or have lived here a lifetime, the Chamber is a one-stop resource: find a business, find a job, find a way to connect. Invest in your community by joining us.

"An engaged Chamber is the difference between a town that grows and a town that simply continues — we exist for the former." — The Chamber Board · Beaver Dam, WI

Programs

The programs that move our local economy forward.

FLAGSHIP PROGRAM · I

Leadership Beaver Dam.

Each year, twenty-five emerging leaders from across the community spend a single day a month immersed in the systems that make Beaver Dam function: city government, healthcare, manufacturing, education, agriculture, public safety, the arts.

By the time the cohort ends, you understand how this town actually works — and you have a network of fellow alumni that lasts a career. Now in its 31st year, the program counts more than 400 alumni currently leading Beaver Dam businesses, boards, and councils.

PROGRAM · II

Manufacturing Business Alliance.

A peer council for Dodge County manufacturers — quarterly roundtables on workforce, automation, supply chain, and apprenticeships. A direct line to county economic development.
