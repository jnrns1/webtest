AFFILIATIONS & PARTNERS

The chamber doesn't work alone.

A small-city chamber gets multiplied by its partners. We coordinate with the city, the county, the regional manufacturing alliance, the state chamber, and the U.S. Chamber — so a Beaver Dam member's voice carries further than Beaver Dam.

CIVIC PARTNERS

The local circle.

The organizations we share a town with — and a calendar with.

CITY GOVERNMENT

City of Beaver Dam

Mayor's office, common council, parks & recreation. Joint planning on downtown, parking, and signature events.

 DOWNTOWN BID

Downtown Beaver Dam

Storefront support, downtown events programming, and Main Street design grants. Shared committee work.

 TOURISM

Visit Beaver Dam

The room-tax-funded tourism arm. Joint promotion of festivals, lake recreation, and the Wisconsin Arts & Peony Festival.

 SCHOOLS

Beaver Dam Unified School District

Youth Apprenticeship pipeline, Internships program, Junior Achievement partnership.
REGIONAL PARTNERS

Dodge County and beyond.

When the issue is bigger than one city, we work the region.

MANUFACTURING

Dodge Region Manufacturing Business Alliance

Peer council for plant managers and HR leads across Dodge County.

 COUNTY

Dodge County

County government, economic development, and tourism (Dodge County Tourism Association).

 EDUCATION

Moraine Park Technical College

Workforce training, apprenticeship pathways, and entrepreneurship programs.
STATE & NATIONAL

A voice in Madison and DC.

Membership in the state and national chambers gives Beaver Dam members a much bigger seat than they could buy on their own.

STATE

Wisconsin Manufacturers & Commerce (WMC)

The state chamber. Policy advocacy at the Capitol, and the WMC Foundation's leadership programs.

 NATIONAL

U.S. Chamber of Commerce

Federal advocacy, small-business resources, and the chambers-of-commerce certification program.

 PROFESSIONAL

Association of Chamber of Commerce Executives

Best-practice exchange with chambers our size around the country.
PARTNERSHIP INQUIRIES

Want to partner?

Nonprofit, civic, or regional — if your work overlaps with ours, we're easy to reach. Most partnerships start with a coffee at the visitor center.
