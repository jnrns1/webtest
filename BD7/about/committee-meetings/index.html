COMMITTEE MEETINGS

Where the work actually gets done.

Most chamber decisions don't happen at the board level — they happen at a 7am committee table with a thermos of coffee and the day's agenda. Members are welcome to sit in on any committee meeting; reach out to RSVP.

HOW IT WORKS

Six committees. One year. Real outcomes.

Each standing committee meets monthly at the chamber office (127 S. Spring St.), reports up to the executive committee, and ships at least one tangible thing per quarter — an event, a policy letter, a partnership, an awards slate.
