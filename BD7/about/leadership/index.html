CHAMBER STAFF

The people answering the phone.

A four-person staff runs the day-to-day. Walk in any weekday and you'll meet at least one of them.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Daphne Rusinow

Executive Director · oversees the chamber's strategy, advocacy, and day-to-day. Started as an ambassador.

EVENTS & MARKETING

Events & Marketing Director

Runs the calendar — from the Taste of Wisconsin to Business After Hours. Also manages the printed Community Guide.

MEMBERSHIP

Member Services

Onboarding new members, managing the directory, and the first call when a business has a question.

VISITOR CENTER

Visitor Center Lead

The friendly face at the front desk. Maps, recommendations, the Community Guide, and a real welcome.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Elected by the membership.

Thirteen local business owners and community leaders set chamber strategy, approve the budget, and represent the membership at city hall and the county.

CHAIR

Chair of the Board

Local business owner · 3-year term · sets the agenda for the executive committee.

VICE CHAIR

Vice Chair

Chairs strategy & finance subcommittees and steps in for the chair as needed.

TREASURER

Treasurer

Reviews the chamber's monthly P&L and works with the auditor on the year-end report.

SECRETARY

Secretary

Keeps the minutes, maintains member records of resolutions, and runs the annual meeting.

PAST CHAIR

Immediate Past Chair

Continuity seat — guides board onboarding and chairs the nominations committee.

DIRECTORS

8 At-Large Directors

Elected from the general membership; each chairs a standing committee.

CHAMBER AMBASSADORS

Volunteers in matching shirts.

If you've been to a Beaver Dam ribbon cutting, you've met them. The Ambassador team is the chamber's volunteer welcome corps — 40+ local business owners, retirees, and recent transplants who give their evenings to the work.

Where the work actually gets done.
