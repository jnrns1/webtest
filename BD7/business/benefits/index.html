MEMBER BENEFITS

Five things membership actually buys.

Most members earn back their dues in the first 12 months — and that's before counting the introduction that turned into a contract, the ribbon cutting that put your face in the paper, or the time someone called the chamber looking for a vendor and got pointed at you.

BENEFIT 01 — NETWORKING

87 events a year. The good ones in matching shirts.

Member-only mixers, ribbon cuttings, breakfast networks, after-hours events. The Bar Crawl committee, the Golf Outing committee, the Annual Awards. The rooms where Beaver Dam business actually gets done.

  • Monthly Business After Hours mixers
  • Tuesday morning Breakfast Network — speaker series
  • Ribbon cuttings hosted by the Ambassador team
  • Member-only signature events (Golf Outing, Bar Crawl)
87
Events / yr
BENEFIT 02 — MARKETING

Your name where 30,000 people already look.

The printed Community Guide goes to hotel rooms, real-estate offices, newcomer welcome bags, and the visitor center. The online directory is searchable by category and the highest-traffic page on this site after the homepage.

  • Printed Community Guide — 30,000 copies / yr
  • Online Member Directory listing & category placement
  • Member-news posts on the chamber's social channels
  • Email-blast sponsorship slots in the chamber newsletter
30k
Community Guides / yr
BENEFIT 03 — ADVOCACY

A voice at city hall — and beyond.

Through our affiliations with the WMC and the U.S. Chamber, your seat at the chamber buys a much bigger seat in Madison and DC than your business could buy alone. Locally, the chamber speaks for members at city council, county board, and the state assembly.

  • Position letters on local zoning, tax, and infrastructure issues
  • Member surveys feeding policy responses
  • Quarterly off-the-record sessions with city leadership
  • Affiliation with WMC + U.S. Chamber for state & federal voice
BENEFIT 04 — PROGRAMS

Six programs included with membership.

Leadership Beaver Dam (tuition-supplemented for members). YPN (free for under-40s on the member roster). Insurance group rates. Manufacturing Alliance. Annual Awards eligibility. Youth Apprenticeship hosting.

BENEFIT 05 — DISCOUNTS

Group rates on the unsexy stuff.

Health insurance, workers' comp, cyber liability, and the chamber's affinity discounts on shipping, payroll, and credit-card processing. Members report 5-figure annual savings on group health alone.

  • Group health, workers' comp, cyber liability
  • Chamber Checks — gift-certificate program with 0% fee
  • Discounted UPS / shipping rates
  • Discounted payroll & merchant services
