10 REASONS

To buy Chamber Checks instead of a generic gift card.

01

Stays in Beaver Dam

Every dollar redeemed lands in a chamber-member business — not a faceless retailer.

02

No fees

Buyers, recipients, and redeeming businesses all pay $0 in fees. The chamber underwrites the program.

03

Never expire

No date on the bottom. Honored at face value forever.

04

Used 200+ places

Restaurants, retail, salons, mechanics, dentists, even some lawn care.

05

Any denomination

$5 to $500. Works for stocking stuffers and corporate gifting alike.

06

Bulk discounts

5% off corporate orders of 50+ checks. Add a custom envelope.

07

Tax-deductible (in some cases)

Employer gifting may be deductible — check with your accountant.

08

Real paper

Numbered, secure, and great-looking. People save them in card boxes.

09

Trackable

Lost one? Look up the balance and reissue at the visitor center.

10 — THE BIG ONE

Keeps the local economy local.

Last year Chamber Checks circulated $420,000 of spending through Beaver Dam member businesses. Every check is a small bet on the town.
