FAQ

Quick answers.

Q

Do I have to be in Beaver Dam?

No. Most members are based in Beaver Dam or Dodge County, but we accept members from anywhere in southeastern Wisconsin who do business in our area.

Q

Can a sole proprietor join?

Yes. Standard membership covers sole proprietors and 1–25 employee businesses.

Q

How fast is onboarding?

Directory listing within 5 business days; ribbon cutting within 30 days.

Q

Are dues prorated?

Yes. New members joining mid-year pay prorated dues for their first 12 months.

Q

Are dues tax-deductible?

For most businesses, yes — as an ordinary business expense. (Talk to your accountant.)

Q

Can I pay monthly?

Manufacturer and Standard tiers can split annual dues into quarterly installments.
