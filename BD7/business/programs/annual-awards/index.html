52ND ANNUAL AWARDS GALA · SEPT 18, 2026

The whole town. One room.

Once a year the chamber pulls out the white tablecloths, dusts off the stage, and recognizes the businesses, leaders, and emerging voices who shaped Beaver Dam over the past twelve months. Black-tie. Members and the public welcome.

DATE

Friday · Sept 18, 2026

Doors 6:00 pm · dinner 7:00 · awards 8:30 · dancing 9:30.

VENUE

Old Hickory Golf Course

Banquet hall · Beaver Dam, WI. Onsite parking and overflow at the chamber lot.

TICKETS

$75 · Member tables $700

Single tickets and 10-top member tables. Sponsor tables include premium seating.

THE FOUR AWARDS

What we recognize.

Nominations open in May; submit through the chamber website. The Annual Awards committee deliberates in July; honorees are announced at the gala.

AWARD 01

Member of the Year

A chamber member business — large or small — that exemplified the chamber's mission this year. Recent recipients have ranged from a 4-person bakery to a 200-employee manufacturer.

AWARD 02

5 Under 40

Five young professionals under 40 making outsized impact in business, civic life, or the chamber. Selected from YPN nominations and open community submissions.

AWARD 03

Innovation

A member business that did something genuinely new — a product, a process, a partnership — that moved Beaver Dam forward.

AWARD 04

Emerging Leader

A community member — chamber or not — early in their leadership journey, recognized for the start of something the town will be glad to remember.
