CHAMBER PROGRAMS

Six programs. One playbook.

Networking is the table-stakes. Programs are how a chamber actually builds its town. The Beaver Dam Chamber runs six — leadership, young professionals, manufacturing, insurance, awards, and youth apprenticeship — each tuned to a specific stage of a career or a specific kind of business.

9-MONTH COHORT

Leadership Beaver Dam

The flagship 9-month immersion. 24 seats per cohort. Behind-the-scenes days inside city hall, the hospital, the schools, the courthouse, and member businesses.

 UNDER 40

Young Professional Network

Monthly socials, civic-engagement nights, and an annual leadership summit. 112 members and growing.

 GROUP RATES

Insurance Programs

Member-only group rates on health, workers' comp, and cyber.

 EVERY SEPTEMBER

Annual Awards Gala

Member of the Year, 5 Under 40, Innovation, and Emerging Leader. The chamber's signature evening — black-tie, members and the public welcome.

 HIGH SCHOOL

Youth Apprenticeship & Internships

Pipeline from BDUSD students into Beaver Dam manufacturers, banks, and trades. Paid placements, school credit.

 PEER COUNCIL

Manufacturing Business Alliance

Plant managers, HR leads, and ops directors across 14 Dodge County manufacturers. Quarterly peer council.
WHO QUALIFIES

Most programs are members-only.

Annual Awards is open to all. Youth Apprenticeship runs through the schools. The other four — Leadership BD, YPN, Insurance, and the Manufacturing Alliance — are reserved for chamber members. If you're not a member yet, that's the first call.
