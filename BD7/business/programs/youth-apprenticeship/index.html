YOUTH APPRENTICESHIP & INTERNSHIPS

Where the next workforce comes from.

A paid pipeline that puts BDUSD juniors and seniors inside Beaver Dam manufacturers, banks, hospitals, and trades — with school credit and industry-recognized certifications. Run by the chamber in partnership with the school district and the Wisconsin DWD.

FOR STUDENTS

Get paid. Get credit. Get a head start.

Wisconsin Youth Apprenticeship is a state-recognized program. You'll work 450+ paid hours in your industry, take related coursework, and graduate with a state-issued certificate of skills.

  • Open to BDUSD juniors & seniors
  • Paid hourly, plus school credit
  • State-issued YA Certificate at completion
  • Direct connection to local employers post-graduation
Apply through BDUSD →
FOR EMPLOYERS

Build your bench. From your hometown.

Hosting a Youth Apprentice or Intern is one of the best long-term workforce moves a Beaver Dam business can make. The chamber handles paperwork, partnerships, and onboarding support.

  • Chamber covers most administrative coordination
  • State-recognized program; tax credits available
  • Internship pathway also available for adult learners
  • Average host conversion to FTE: 38%
