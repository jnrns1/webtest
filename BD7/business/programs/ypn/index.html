YPN · UNDER 40

The town's next bench of leaders.

YPN is the chamber's under-40 program. We meet monthly for a social, quarterly for civic-engagement nights, and once a year for a full-day leadership summit. The vibe is unstuffy. Bring a friend.

WHAT YPN DOES

Social. Civic. Professional.

SOCIAL

Monthly Socials

Lakeside patios, downtown wine bars, the bowling alley. The point is to know each other before there's a deal on the table.

CIVIC

Civic Engagement

Quarterly off-the-record sessions with city & county leaders. Not lobbying — listening, then asking better questions.

PROFESSIONAL

Annual Summit

Full-day workshop in July. Real speakers, real workshops, real time on each other's calendars.

MEMBER SPOTLIGHT

YPN is how I built every professional relationship I have in this town. Bar Crawl committee one year, leadership summit speaker the next.
