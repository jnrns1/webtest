SPONSORSHIP

Three tiers. One town grateful.

Sponsor dollars are how the chamber multiplies its budget — and how a small-city chamber pulls off a 300-person Annual Awards Gala, a printed 30,000-copy Community Guide, and the Taste of Wisconsin every June. Sponsorships start at $500 and run to $25,000.

TIER 01 — EXECUTIVE CIRCLE

$10,000 – $25,000 / yr

The top of the masthead. Logo on every chamber asset for the year, premium event presence, sponsor-branded program slot, and a dedicated chamber introduction to ten member businesses of your choice.

  • Logo on chamber homepage, every email blast, and the Community Guide cover
  • Premium table at every signature event (Gala, Bar Crawl, Golf Outing, Taste of Wisconsin)
  • One sponsor-branded program (e.g. "Leadership Beaver Dam, presented by …")
  • Annual board dinner invitation
  • Custom chamber-led intro program (10 hand-picked introductions)
$25k
Top tier · 4 seats per year
TIER 02 — VISION

$5,000 – $9,999 / yr

A strong public partner of the chamber. Logo placement across signature events, recognition in the printed Community Guide, and reserved seating at the Annual Awards Gala.

  • Logo on signature event signage & recap emails
  • Half-page recognition in the printed Community Guide
  • Reserved 4-top at the Annual Awards Gala
  • Member-spotlight feature in the chamber newsletter (1 per year)
$9k
Mid tier
TIER 03 — MISSION

$500 – $4,999 / yr

Built for small businesses underwriting one event, one program, or one annual newsletter slot. Pick your audience, pick your level. Many members start here and graduate to Vision over time.

  • Sponsor recognition at one signature event of choice
  • Quarter-page recognition in the Community Guide
  • Two tickets to the Annual Awards Gala
  • Logo on the sponsor wall at the visitor center for the year
$500
Entry point
