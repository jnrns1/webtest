DISCOVER BEAVER DAM

A small city with a whole lot to love.

From sunset on the lake to fireworks on the Fourth, our town has its own rhythm — slower, friendlier, and worth the drive. Stop by the Visitor Center for maps, tips, and a real welcome.

PARKS & THE LAKE

Trails, pavilions, and one of Wisconsin's best inland lakes.

Waterfront walking paths, picnic spots, and the kind of mornings that make families come back for a second weekend.
SUNSETS & SEASONS

Autumn color on the water, summer on the patio, winter lights downtown.

We lean into every season. Bring a camera — and a sweater for those Wisconsin evenings.

 DOWNTOWN

Shops, cafes, and the gazebo on the square.

A walkable downtown built for Saturday mornings, Friday-night strolls, and that one good cup of coffee.
BEAVER DAM BY THE NUMBERS

A lake, a downtown, and 87 reasons a year to stop by.

Festivals, parades, farmers markets, fireworks, parties on the patio — the calendar is the heartbeat of the town.

FAMILY-FRIENDLY

Playgrounds, fountains, and the Mini Boss kids' club.

Built for kids, embraced by grown-ups. Bring the wagon and stay for the ice cream.

 ARTS & CULTURE

Community theatre, public art, and a music scene that punches up.

Art on the Town, the Beaver Dam Area Community Theatre, and a year of small stages worth showing up for.

 EAT & DRINK

Wisconsin supper clubs, craft brewers, and small-town diners.

From cheese curds to Friday fish fry — the Chamber's member directory keeps the good ones easy to find.

Beaver Dam is a city you can settle into without giving up your nature, your downtown, or your weekends. Whether you're stopping by or staying for good, the Visitor Center is your first stop.
