Event Details

Beaver Dam Wine Tour

Get ready to sip, stroll, and explore during one of Downtown Beaver Dam’s most anticipated events of the year — the Beaver Dam Wine Tour!

Participants will enjoy an evening of wine tasting while visiting a variety of local businesses throughout downtown. It’s the perfect opportunity to discover new favorites, connect with friends, and experience all that our vibrant downtown has to offer.

Your registration includes:

  • A souvenir wine glass

  • A wristband indicating you are 21+

  • Wine samples at participating locations

  • Raffle entries for prizes

Early bird pricing will be available for a limited time, so be sure to register early!

Designated Driver tickets will also be available and include non-alcoholic refreshments at select locations.

Whether you’re a longtime wine tour fan or attending for the first time, this event offers a fun, relaxed way to support local businesses and enjoy an evening out in Downtown Beaver Dam.

More details — including participating locations, event features, and ticket information — will be announced closer to the event.

How It Works And
How To Start

There are 20+ Sip Stops on the Wine Tour. Each Sip Stop will have samples available, including one “Sip Stop” being a hydration station. Alcoholic sample sizes are limited to 1 ounce. A map will be given to you to mark the designated stops. Participants pick up their glass and maps at the Beaver Dam Chamber & Visitor Center and bring their glass along to participating business to sample. Attendees choose their own path along the tour and select which stops that they would like to visit.

To start the Wine Tour, each ticket holder must stop at the main check in location – Beaver Dam & Visitor Center between 11 am – 7:00 pm on the day of the event to show their ID (proof of legal drinking age) and to pick up their wristband, souvenir glass, and map.

No samples will be poured prior to 5:30 pm. No ID = No Wristband; No wristband = No wine. You may NOT pick up wristbands for other people. Wristbands will be secured at check in. No exceptions. Wristbands must be worn before leaving the Chamber during check in. 

Timeline

11 am:  Registration opens on Friday
5:30 pm Sampling begins
8:30 pm Sampling ends

Rules

Must be 21+
Must provide proof of legal age at wristband check in.
No wine/beverages will be served prior to 5:30 pm.

Please do not ask participating businesses to over pour or give you an extra swig. It’s not worth breaking the over serving law. If you appear intoxicated, business owners and servers have the right to refuse serving you.

Tickets

$45($35 Early Bird) per person. 
$10 Designated Driver Tickets (infused water and snacks)
Limited to 400 tickets.
Must be 21 to consume alcoholic beverages.

Tickets are non-refundable.

Tips

Bring your ID.
Wear comfortable shoes and weather-appropriate clothing, you’ll be walking outdoors between locations rain or shine.
Cross the street at crosswalks!
Post photos to Instagram and Facebook using hashtag #BDWineTour Event happens rain or shine. Tickets are nonrefundable.
