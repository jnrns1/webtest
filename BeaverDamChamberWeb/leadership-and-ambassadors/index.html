Leadership and Ambassadors

Beaver Dam Chamber Leadership

We’re proud to have our business community represented by an outstanding team of leaders, including our dedicated Board of Directors and passionate Ambassadors. Together, they bring energy, vision, and expertise to the Beaver Dam Chamber & Visitor Center.

Our Board of Directors focuses on understanding member needs and setting the strategic direction of the Chamber. Their leadership ensures we address key issues, support local businesses, and make Beaver Dam a great place to live, work, and visit.

Our Ambassadors, known for their iconic green jackets, are the welcoming face of the Chamber. These volunteers connect with members, serve as community liaisons, and energize every event with their enthusiasm. They play a vital role in building relationships, recruiting new members, and promoting the businesses that make our community thrive.

Together, our Board and Ambassadors strengthen the Chamber’s mission to foster growth and create a vibrant, connected Beaver Dam community.
