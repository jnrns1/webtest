The Original Craft Beer & Cheese Festival

FAQs

Everything you need to know about the Taste.

Featured Craft Brewers

Get ready to raise your glass at Taste of Wisconsin with an amazing lineup of local craft brewers! From bold IPAs to smooth lagers, sours, stouts and unique seasonal flavors, there’s something for every beer lover to sip and savor.

Featured Wine & Cheese

Treat your taste buds to the perfect pairing of Wisconsin wines (5 Wineries) and 40+ cheeses at Taste of Wisconsin! Sip on locally crafted reds, whites, and fruit blends while sampling rich, flavorful cheeses from the heart of dairy country. It’s a match made in Midwest heaven!.

Mark Your Calendars!

Saturday, June 20, 2026 | Noon to 4 PM

Experience the Original Taste of Wisconsin: Beer, Cheese & Wine Festival

Get ready to immerse yourself in a true Wisconsin tradition at the Original Taste of Wisconsin: Beer, Cheese & Wine Festival in Beaver Dam! This highly anticipated event returns on Saturday, June 20, 2026, offering a day filled with exceptional flavors, live entertainment, and unforgettable memories. Your ticket includes unlimited sampling of over 100+ craft beers, more than 30 varieties of delicious cheese, and a curated selection of local wines. To enhance your experience, you’ll receive a souvenir tasting glass and access to our Taste of Wisconsin festival app to guide your culinary adventure.

Adding to the excitement, Milwaukee’s own Mood Medication will take the stage, delivering an electrifying mix of hits from Green Day, Blink-182, The Killers, and more. Pair your tastings with mouthwatering options from local food trucks, and test your skills with games like life-size Jenga, Hammerschlagen, and Masskrugstemmen, a Bavarian stein-holding competition.

Take advantage of exclusive offers, including a $10 discount on Early Bird Tickets and our Six-Pack Special, where you can buy six tickets for the price of five. This special offers only lasts through May 1, 2026 and tickets are limited!

This event truly offers something for everyone, blending Wisconsin’s love of craft beer, cheese, and wine with lively entertainment and engaging activities. 

Brewery Pop-Up Ticket Events | Taste of Wisconsin

Taste of Wisconsin is teaming up with Wisconsin craft breweries for a series of one-day pop-up ticket events, giving beer lovers a chance to save money, skip online fees, and enjoy exceptional craft beer while securing festival tickets. A limited number of paper tickets will be available at each pop-up with Early Bird pricing of $45 per ticket or the popular Six Pack Special for $225 (buy five, get one free). Early Bird pricing is available through May 1, after which ticket prices increase by $10.

Every ticket purchased at a brewery pop-up event is entered to win $100 cash, courtesy of Klodowski Real Estate. Cash and credit cards are accepted; credit card purchases will incur fees. All onsite purchases qualify for the raffle, including credit card transactions, and winners do not need to be present to win. Tickets must be purchased onsite at the pop-up event to be eligible. 

Ooga Brewing Company Pop-Up + Bockfest

Saturday, March 21, 2026 |  5:00–7:00 PM
Ooga Bockfest: Noon–10:00 PM

The first pop-up takes place at Ooga Brewing Company during their annual Bockfest, a full-day celebration of malt-forward lagers and the arrival of spring. Bockfest features the annual tapping of the Roaming Goat, live music, and bierstacheln (beer poking) — a traditional German technique where a heated iron caramelizes the beer’s sugars, creating a smoother pour with rich caramel and toffee notes. It’s a can’t-miss experience for fans of darker beers and classic brewing traditions.

Ooga Brewing Company is known for its creative yet approachable craft beers and strong community roots in Beaver Dam. As a Taste of Wisconsin festival vendor, Ooga brings expertly brewed styles that celebrate both tradition and innovation — making Bockfest the perfect backdrop for this pop-up ticket event.

Ope Brewing Company Pop-Up Ticket Event

Saturday, April 4, 2026 | 2:00–5:00 PM

The second pop-up heads to Ope Brewing Company, offering another opportunity to purchase limited paper tickets, avoid online fees, and enjoy fresh craft beer straight from the taproom while locking in Early Bird pricing. Enjoy a DJ from 3 pm – 7 pm and live music after. 

Ope Brewing Company is a proud Wisconsin craft brewery and Taste of Wisconsin vendor, known for producing quality beers with broad appeal and local pride. Hosting a pop-up at Ope Brewing allows festival fans to connect directly with the breweries they’ll see pouring at the event.

Hoya Hop House Pop-Up Ticket Event

Saturday, April 18, 2026 | 3:00–5:00 PM

The next pop-up heads to Hoya Hop House Brewing, giving festival fans another chance to purchase limited paper tickets, skip online fees, and enjoy craft beer straight from the taproom while locking in Early Bird pricing for the Taste of Wisconsin Beer, Cheese & Wine Fest. Stick around after the pop-up for live music from 6:00–9:00 PM, making it the perfect night to grab tickets, grab a beer, and enjoy the local music scene.

Hoya Hop House Brewing crafts exceptional beer, cocktails, and food in a vibrant, nature-inspired taproom that nurtures community and creativity. Their welcoming space — complete with a rotating lineup of handcrafted beers, seasonal offerings, and experimental small-batch releases — offers something for every palate.
